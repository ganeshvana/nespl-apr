SO Alternative Products

This module benifits you a lot when it comes to adding and providing a list of product alternatives for your own personnel to work on. You can alternate products through an added 'Alternateed Products' table to your Sale Order page and an 'Alternatives' button to your List.

Features

    You can create your products alternative (Substitute Products) to your product, and choose the product you need

Screenshots

Select all products alternative to your product : Go To Products-->Products-->Alternative

Open list the products alternative in Sale Order : Go To Sales --> Orders --> Quotations --> you can choose the product you need by clicking the 'Add to order lines' button

list products alternative

Add contract type in employee contract at the field given as 'Employee Category'

If you want to replace one of the products from the List, then click on the 'Alternatives' button and choose the designated desired replacement and click on 'Replace'.
